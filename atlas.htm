Project Atlas

Project Atlas is my attempt at designing a modular natcar that works as an educational tool and can be easily improved and redesigned by other students. The project consists of an analog front end to detect a signal, a full featured BLDC motor controller, Power system, and microcontroller board.

Analog Front End

The analog frontend is an LC Tank with a resonant frequency of 75khz. Filtering and Gain control is necessary to process the received signal from the LC tank into a DC analog signal that can be read by an onboard ADC.

BLDC Motor Controller

The BLDC Motor controller is a board that allows for full featured independent control of a BLDC motor

Power Board

The power board monitors the battery's voltage and current usage levels and provides the microcontroller with safe guards in the event of a low-battery or short in the system.

Microcontroller Board

The microcontroller board provides lead-ins to various sensors and inputs. The main control board will consist mainly of connectors, displays, and auxillary ports for sistor boards.
